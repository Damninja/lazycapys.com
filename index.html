Our Story

Why a capybara?

Capybaras are nature's most unbothered creatures. They share their hot springs with everyone, get along with any animal, and radiate a calm that the internet has rightfully become obsessed with.

Lazy Capys started as a love letter to that energy. We believe everyone deserves a little capybara in their life — whether it's a plushie on your desk, a cozy hoodie, or a mug that reminds you to slow down and just vibe.

Every product is designed with care, printed on demand through Printful, and shipped in eco-friendly packaging. We're a small, capy-loving operation, and we're so glad you're here.
